Double-Booking Detector
How Double Booking Works
A fraudulent broker takes your load and re-posts it on multiple load boards under a different MC number. The original carrier picks up the freight. A second "carrier" shows up to steal it. By the time you figure it out, the cargo is gone and you owe the shipper.

LoadVerify scans DAT, Truckstop, and private boards in real-time, cross-referencing origin, destination, pickup window, and commodity to detect the same load posted in multiple places simultaneously.
